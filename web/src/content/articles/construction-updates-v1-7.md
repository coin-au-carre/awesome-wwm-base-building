## Rotate your component on 2 axis now! [PC only]

Hold **Ctrl** and scroll your mouse wheel to rotate a component on a second axis before placing it.

## Component cap limits increased

### For guild base

- Global cap increased by **10,000 components**
- Density cap (local area) increased by **10,000 components**

<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1509461162520219688/1512385750044250172/image.png?ex=6a23e67d&is=6a2294fd&hm=d6fe8734b7d5c6cd82010fdde6f12469189e9cf14fc1d10bf5a469f5a2f1b076&" alt="Component cap limits in guild base" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 40%;" />

### For solo base

- No limitation anymore. The density cap has been lifted entirely.

## New v1.7 update components

The Kaifeng fresh zone brought new components in 2 subcategories under `Guild Construction`: **Large Component** and **Structures**.

Note: all new `Large Component` items can be placed in your guild base, but **only once each**.

## Change time in guild base

You can now change the time of day in your guild base, just like in solo. Go to the menu and access the clock.

- Requires the **Management** permission.
- The time change applies to everyone in the guild.

<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1509461162520219688/1512385638760976424/image.png?ex=6a23e663&is=6a2294e3&hm=dad0522b951d47ce4bfaa753c6ff3a003b5fee48e001ac188a61ec866fec1b2a&" alt="Clock menu to change time in guild base" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 60%;" />

## Other Updates

- Can tag components in the Favorites tab
- Can edit components in guild base: move, replace, rotate
- 3 different cameras in build mode: classic, free move, above view
- Can search components by name

## Bugs

Many bugs have been reported with the update. We keep track of a list of bugs here: <https://www.wherebuildersmeet.com/bugs/>. Help us report. 

Avoid guild base wipe and careful when removing components! Some components (e.g. from guild construction diagrams) cannot be placed as of now leading to cause issues.

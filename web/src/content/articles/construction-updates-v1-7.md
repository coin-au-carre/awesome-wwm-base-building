## Rotate your component on 2 axis now! [PC only]

Hold **Ctrl** and scroll your mouse wheel to rotate a component on a second axis before placing it.

## Component cap limits increased

### For guild base

- Global cap increased by **10,000 components**
- Density cap (local area) increased by **10,000 components**

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1509461162520219688/1512385750044250172/image.png?ex=6a23e67d&format=webp&hm=d6fe8734b7d5c6cd82010fdde6f12469189e9cf14fc1d10bf5a469f5a2f1b076&is=6a2294fd&quality=lossless" alt="Component cap limits in guild base" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 40%;" />

### For solo base

- No more limitations. The density cap has been lifted entirely.

## Change time in guild base

You can now change the time of day in your guild base, just like in solo. Go to the menu and access the clock.

- Requires the **Management** permission.
- The time change applies to everyone in the guild.

## Understanding the Copyright system [Guild and Solo]

*Thanks to [AegisNite Edge](/builders/aegisnite-edge) and [Crueliet](/builders/crueliet) for their help to assist me figuring this out.*

To save a diagram in both guild and solo base, you need to own at least **70%** of the components in it, meaning you placed them yourself. If the Copyright Score is above **30%**, you cannot save the diagram.

This can be a harsh limitation in collaborative builds. For example, if two builders split work 69% and 31%, neither of them can save the diagram since neither reaches the 70% threshold alone.

## Other useful updates

- You can tag components in the `Favorites` tab
- You can edit components in guild base: move, replace, rotate
- You now can choose 3 different cameras in build mode: classic, free move, above view
- You can search components by name

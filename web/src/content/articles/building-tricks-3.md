## 4. Fill the open gate in the guild party area
<p class="not-prose -mt-4 mb-6 text-xs text-muted-foreground">by <a href="/builders/alscend" class="font-semibold text-foreground/80 hover:text-primary underline underline-offset-2 transition-colors">alscend</a></p>

The guild party area has an open gate that leads directly into the guild wall, which can feel awkward. Placing a gate in it closes the space and makes it feel more cozy.

<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1483447711499030634/1501743602877862019/20265783053.png?ex=6a10f5b7&is=6a0fa437&hm=b088a07db4bedc786be2bbc846461beaa29b909b9775a98485aeb8252ec6a36e&" alt="Open gate in the guild party area before placing a gate — opens to nothing but the guild wall" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1483447711499030634/1501743603360464996/20265782714.png?ex=6a10f5b8&is=6a0fa438&hm=dd9d8f3b600812b412f5310b35ea27245d55e84df4e5ee639eee5673eb9456c4&" alt="Same gate area after — a gate fills the opening and closes the space" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

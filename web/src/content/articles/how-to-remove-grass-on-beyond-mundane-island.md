**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a559d5f&format=webp&hm=ba1b3d3532ac3cc49be3c3032a75874e05a56f6cc013f2d6a6f54eb0ec823ac7&is=6a544bdf&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex ***without leaving a single trace behind***

**OPTION 1: REMOVE A LARGE AREA WITH ONLY ONE COMPONENT**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a559f48&format=webp&hm=735a82802816c839bc9aba142c836c0db28e90ba4cc04cdfa2de021363048a00&is=6a544dc8&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Make sure to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so...

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a559ffc&format=webp&hm=bc79919e552c264170e6bb0291c95484c3865e7dbef11a4ccf51bf676ff65ed2&is=6a544e7c&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a55a295&format=webp&hm=fc43d8f43ba4e7337d49f1d88e7d99e1a369f94ab4af822840a6907e30200871&is=6a545115&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Standard Inns can bug out and would not clear the grass some times

---

Grand Inns seem to work consistently

---

If it bugs out, you can adjust the building with Move tool and hope it gets fixed or start over from the beginning

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a55a456&format=webp&hm=22921abbc84a3ab79f42ce7a5e115d9dffab0d4fe4feb4e1549007fd1af6f810&is=6a5452d6&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a55a477&format=webp&hm=2d18c4be8abc7731fa0d2486f93b2b7b130329e86ad9c23d8fcc4cd0d8e23b7d&is=6a5452f7&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a55a595&format=webp&hm=4734cb7fdebbef52f616294816abde626a2493cfc51b07e22a42d2f56bf1b41d&is=6a545415&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a55a642&format=webp&hm=873205d481396059c343f3d7ca11fcf442204ac18c5bc7ea08c18f856a1d22f1&is=6a5454c2&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Maybe some other buildings work better than the inns.

---

**OPTION 2: REMOVE A SMALL OR SPECIFIC AREA**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a piece of "Indoor Flooring"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526227849818804365/1.png?ex=6a5641f3&format=webp&hm=cbebbd300613f1ccc4736473a429bcd675d346dbe834847922c0c8874bc23f9c&is=6a54f073&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Rotate it 45 degree

---

If using the larger piece, make sure the wood grain runs along North-South

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526228525823295679/3.png?ex=6a564294&format=webp&hm=e6d9d5c4c8c28a2c9ac2b71c209cea5103735afd661f669e7c9ed95d89c3ff53&is=6a54f114&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

The wood gain's orientation of the small piece does not matter

---

**STEP 4:**

---

**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a5e2f1f&format=webp&hm=63cf83511a34016dd3a099100a0359b8d06138d351c343ca98388015c4e749b0&is=6a5cdd9f&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex ***without leaving a single trace behind***

**OPTION 1: REMOVE A LARGE AREA WITH ONLY ONE COMPONENT**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a5e3108&format=webp&hm=745a6f18de975d16ac364f36177bb90a843d2f99c91f3b57b84051144a41be46&is=6a5cdf88&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

May have to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so WITH THE **FRONT** FACING DOWNWARD IF USING A GRAND INN or WITH THE **BACK** FACING DOWNWARD IF USING A STANDARD INN

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a5e31bc&format=webp&hm=2c251a5b3e3d3efc0d0c346cac86e2d844e1635609c39a277020b2e8ee7c2751&is=6a5ce03c&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a5e3455&format=webp&hm=1053b1ee3000de6a8e5ab78fe61274edbd62ca6e24fd3e92b97c47971d5e3e15&is=6a5ce2d5&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

If you see some parts of the inn sticking out or grass are left behind, go back to STEP 2 and make sure the orientation of the building is correct

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a5e3616&format=webp&hm=d2f52c8ecfc5dfc95597a110092a99adf30587a77128d727b4193b6fd6f6ddc5&is=6a5ce496&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a5e3637&format=webp&hm=a4361f9b1028f2c6f95cbb85563efd4b549c62b9065d1a3e31f7a39f0f557b50&is=6a5ce4b7&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a5e3755&format=webp&hm=cfefecf87a00992f851f05cec249ad31aa78f6194dc6905d202c40b5e4edce00&is=6a5ce5d5&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a5e3802&format=webp&hm=e3d527b8068622da2c6d15e784fb6f97df4d84367a07e7a093faebdbf8a33923&is=6a5ce682&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Another way to quickly glitch your build camera under the ground is building a standard inn while in free camera mode, wait for it to load completely and pushes your camera under the ground.

---

**OPTION 2: REMOVE A SMALL OR SPECIFIC AREA**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a piece of "Indoor Flooring"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526227849818804365/1.png?ex=6a5e2af3&format=webp&hm=63c3ff461c8ec6d0421d7b3943411061973f95e05296d35cfa5d14cc6823224a&is=6a5cd973&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Rotate it 45 degree

---

If using the larger piece, make sure the wood grain runs along North-South

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526228525823295679/3.png?ex=6a5e2b94&format=webp&hm=d60dc8e1cc7d146e538da675181f34631fb3cc412a6571cca0f3de0d5cb72e06&is=6a5cda14&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

The wood gain's orientation of the small piece does not matter

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sink the correctly rotated flooring tile into the ground

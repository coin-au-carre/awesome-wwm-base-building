**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a52519f&format=webp&hm=ff3297835c936851a9593ba78096b4829ffe3a812cd3a814c42bd847520891c7&is=6a51001f&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a525388&format=webp&hm=846dc7da0b51eb54615ad2e34b8ac371636d7a9c22d2b9a1fee42559a30cd521&is=6a510208&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Make sure to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so...

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a52543c&format=webp&hm=4b352ddfc5d4d85660260864ac11f4bb4acad7a7abaa4d5aaea6dc9a7c468e17&is=6a5102bc&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a5256d5&format=webp&hm=5b39213f53ab476e6696abf839f62f4fee91d2393af1770e8529ad3b56d8e914&is=6a510555&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Standard Inns can bug out and would not clear the grass some times

---

Grand Inns seem to work consistently

---

If it bugs out, you can adjust the building with Move tool and hope it gets fixed or start over from the beginning

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a525896&format=webp&hm=1448ca06e7001e2fedb276d7fc0e1a8c2e9b269b4880e1afcf6201c6759294fc&is=6a510716&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a5258b7&format=webp&hm=0a2555af017be68d54080544f5467c63999fdde5075f5d7b3874e4f3dcaef560&is=6a510737&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a5259d5&format=webp&hm=161d75926012e7d0fa6e70611d6ec901f662b3c953d6a6db0eb810ebe44c8b70&is=6a510855&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a525a82&format=webp&hm=9c2b1ec5e597720d73973a20e97693c34fd397e5c0436310817e37042db941b1&is=6a510902&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Maybe some other buildings work better than the inns.

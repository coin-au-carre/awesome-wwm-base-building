**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a5ed7df&format=webp&hm=3e51984c85ae4c989bb3578fcbe421e6a26547004ce4ba4f72d80ac3cb882ef2&is=6a5d865f&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex ***without leaving a single trace behind***

**OPTION 1: REMOVE A LARGE AREA WITH ONLY ONE COMPONENT**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a5ed9c8&format=webp&hm=89305f314cecdf4437308a52aeb0031d4346d3b7c791ff6fc770d52d697ffa4f&is=6a5d8848&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

May have to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so WITH THE **FRONT** FACING DOWNWARD IF USING A GRAND INN or WITH THE **BACK** FACING DOWNWARD IF USING A STANDARD INN

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a5eda7c&format=webp&hm=89736e9512361e1683cb5ad782f43acf2d8a730e9e7ae370f1a6ce71906b0617&is=6a5d88fc&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a5edd15&format=webp&hm=6564ba1b7940cab8f8d73d2526a80cc91c1f1d636c9449c6d44c034d22e5e02b&is=6a5d8b95&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

If you see some parts of the inn sticking out or grass are left behind, go back to STEP 2 and make sure the orientation of the building is correct

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a5eded6&format=webp&hm=702003ef302b10b865d1eeed118413af763108ea0484cccfc1b3895ed9fc17d0&is=6a5d8d56&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a5edef7&format=webp&hm=8a7f08486b2e465c84006114cee9f4f929b8506765031522b98181ed2e76c77e&is=6a5d8d77&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a5ee015&format=webp&hm=a27c6eb40274abc4770a2b0288c1031e64810de6e0809f256f864031eb1312c1&is=6a5d8e95&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a5ee0c2&format=webp&hm=e96439644477699e76cc8fc979e84ee5fe07d0d4070c2965478708bece6df0c0&is=6a5d8f42&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Another way to quickly glitch your build camera under the ground is building a standard inn while in free camera mode, wait for it to load completely and pushes your camera under the ground.

---

**OPTION 2: REMOVE A SMALL OR SPECIFIC AREA**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a piece of "Indoor Flooring"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526227849818804365/1.png?ex=6a5ed3b3&format=webp&hm=72d6d3625763a557a60d0c5af4355f96328a61b39a576759b76413fea791e0ac&is=6a5d8233&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Rotate it 45 degree

---

If using the larger piece, make sure the wood grain runs along North-South

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526228525823295679/3.png?ex=6a5ed454&format=webp&hm=74aa71214e6054ff848a4eb632a0bd3f7c5bd6711f04a93c2a01ac505492b009&is=6a5d82d4&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

The wood gain's orientation of the small piece does not matter

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sink the correctly rotated flooring tile into the ground

**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a51001f&format=webp&hm=5591f5865cdd71c224ed855fa5b7db167cd690f0eb54b01445e08c615853e1a4&is=6a4fae9f&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a510208&format=webp&hm=bdd89a3d956625e2e54095767f970cecf73477447f0f6cf0974c30ef9f2cfb40&is=6a4fb088&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Make sure to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so...

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a5102bc&format=webp&hm=24388b8571813d41d854337e8b739416bb37b8aa8e5e0f0945df4eaadfe0cc9c&is=6a4fb13c&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a510555&format=webp&hm=25d349e04cdb6e6d1d3fbf2e68c9b96d48c35d1789543f46c5031f35ca5b429a&is=6a4fb3d5&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Standard Inns can bug out and would not clear the grass some times

---

Grand Inns seem to work consistently

---

If it bugs out, you can adjust the building with Move tool and hope it gets fixed or start over from the beginning

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a510716&format=webp&hm=a4608ea75d3fbd9f4069e7f7a13ff9bd39bad2cd0ddd091132051769bb9c5267&is=6a4fb596&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a510737&format=webp&hm=7f240bb4bac995fff538db6667c38f74ebea7fa28caf9123ecc79227d4dde05d&is=6a4fb5b7&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a510855&format=webp&hm=657f852a87d12093b9b3ff821f8419297d542cd45749eb7d41c0f4e857d40873&is=6a4fb6d5&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a510902&format=webp&hm=4471cb9e96e5220b9dcc355f9e801c8323c1a3ba8c667ce604baf4732cd0ebec&is=6a4fb782&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Maybe some other buildings work better than the inns.

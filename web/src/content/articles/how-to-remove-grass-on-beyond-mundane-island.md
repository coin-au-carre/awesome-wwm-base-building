**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a51a8df&format=webp&hm=3740e62d5d160b385dce6aa2b4b23a23b395b732b3871b36a8180eacfe0fde50&is=6a50575f&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a51aac8&format=webp&hm=f7b1d62ded3176375f1d18859ff691fd3d7020013144850caf0a8e0749bbaf5d&is=6a505948&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Make sure to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so...

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a51ab7c&format=webp&hm=c40fb0210d8c687e16bb1551aeb62aecbf32a75687854fa56f1baaab41813012&is=6a5059fc&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a51ae15&format=webp&hm=06436c32c92efcd82ca61d277aec5155b21e3612f87527c6351d700faeee6a3d&is=6a505c95&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Standard Inns can bug out and would not clear the grass some times

---

Grand Inns seem to work consistently

---

If it bugs out, you can adjust the building with Move tool and hope it gets fixed or start over from the beginning

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a51afd6&format=webp&hm=c853adb31e80e0a1e52d22100a01645b2d2f9089fd796560edfe5f32d7bc4781&is=6a505e56&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a51aff7&format=webp&hm=07581908d83661f92178c014c8abf593fefdf8bc4369b1c140733dcab70d22bc&is=6a505e77&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a51b115&format=webp&hm=d2535e2543a0e12663d9894a11cac595a27b1fb66cb02bfaeb263febd505915a&is=6a505f95&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a51b1c2&format=webp&hm=3f6887851f14c4df3ab49500b9a62b120a154b94a8f5ee4ce124f9e1286d9c96&is=6a506042&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Maybe some other buildings work better than the inns.

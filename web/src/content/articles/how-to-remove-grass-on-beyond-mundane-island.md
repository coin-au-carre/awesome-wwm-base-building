**How to remove grass on Beyond Mundane island**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524058003110887535/202677221244.png?ex=6a60d21f&format=webp&hm=1dec32b378a9b01874c172678aa8bbd4e69c0ad1dc7facad07bb3db1475f720e&is=6a5f809f&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

It has come to my attention that you and the lawn are having some emotional friction. I’ve heard your cries, and I promise the grass won't hurt your feelings anymore. Mostly because you actually have to go outside to touch it.

---

Now let’s scrub the grass out of your life like a bad ex ***without leaving a single trace behind***

**OPTION 1: REMOVE A LARGE AREA WITH ONLY ONE COMPONENT**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a large building. Preferably a "standard inn" or a "grand inn"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060054108770314/202677222635.png?ex=6a60d408&format=webp&hm=b06281bc25de1337b63b8517750d689bbc39161055e751a2ef7506d7deadaa98&is=6a5f8288&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

May have to place down the building first so the grass clearing feature is active.

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Pick it up and rotate it 45 degree like so WITH THE **FRONT** FACING DOWNWARD IF USING A GRAND INN or WITH THE **BACK** FACING DOWNWARD IF USING A STANDARD INN

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524060808252751902/202677223241.png?ex=6a60d4bc&format=webp&hm=6aabf11b1e78e1135b9b6d6033a1851ff48ebfb7ce3a593c30624c974f6ce8aa&is=6a5f833c&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Use the Move tool and bury it until the very tip of the structure is just right below the ground

---

**The result:**

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524063599990014032/202677224417.png?ex=6a602e95&format=webp&hm=25e275b0a4a6a5dbfd82a30ff24ef29f7011ca65b5757a58e98f7ecf2854de86&is=6a5edd15&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

If you see some parts of the inn sticking out or grass are left behind, go back to STEP 2 and make sure the orientation of the building is correct

---

The next question you might be asking is "How do I remove the inns now with them buried under the ground?"

---

The answer: Glitch yourself under the lovely grass which you just flattened so you can target the buildings

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a "Rain-Listening Chair"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065480237781032/image.png?ex=6a603056&format=webp&hm=9ecda5364f92af9a974473b87dbbedabbc57746c1efa82a3d8c2d82286d564cc&is=6a5eded6&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Sink it into the ground until only a little bit of it is left

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524065618725179492/202677221643.png?ex=6a603077&format=webp&hm=6d30b6ae15cd26bcda20b93e1a6beadd3bdf4f594c6fe61c9da4787b8411edbb&is=6a5edef7&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 3:**

---

Build something to catch yourself when you go below ground

---

Like this piece of roof. It is rotated 45 degree for better catching action

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524066820040949791/20267722634.png?ex=6a603195&format=webp&hm=e238551147dd8cf4681ac097595677ea28999098f0323202f15932a74c22f905&is=6a5ee015&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

Bury most of that roof to catch yourself

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sit on the buried chair and get off of it

---

You should now be under the ground

---

**STEP 5:**

---

Switch to Follow Camera so your build camera ends up under the ground and not above it

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1524067546788134933/b5.png?ex=6a603242&format=webp&hm=b8c709a0fa85397ea2bef471ac1e7fb7d492900bc12543e56fa19c96ff63f277&is=6a5ee0c2&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

And that conclude on how to effectively remove grass and glitch yourself under the ground on Beyond Mundane island. You may try placing other buildings to remove a smaller area of grass. Another way to quickly glitch your build camera under the ground is building a standard inn while in free camera mode, wait for it to load completely and pushes your camera under the ground.

---

**OPTION 2: REMOVE A SMALL OR SPECIFIC AREA**

---

**STEP 1:**

---

Build a piece of "Indoor Flooring"

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526227849818804365/1.png?ex=6a60cdf3&format=webp&hm=1ebddb74561f4e7f0114c42275ed8df03a36befa69f4a9167806a6f9aa56a93d&is=6a5f7c73&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

**STEP 2:**

---

Rotate it 45 degree

---

If using the larger piece, make sure the wood grain runs along North-South

---

<img src="https://media.discordapp.net/attachments/1524057838614216704/1526228525823295679/3.png?ex=6a60ce94&format=webp&hm=7407e48a8a895bbaa8b4e6ac675e2aae0e51dc121f39ece7f79886e494274de5&is=6a5f7d14&quality=lossless" alt="" style="border-radius: 0.75rem; width: 100%;" />

---

The wood gain's orientation of the small piece does not matter

---

**STEP 4:**

---

Sink the correctly rotated flooring tile into the ground

## 1. Favorites tab

Add your most-used materials to a favorites tab for instant access. No more scrolling through long lists.

---

## 2. Night lighting in guild

Toggle lighting while building in guild mode so you can see what you're doing at night. On Global, this is currently only available in solo worlds.

---

## 3. Drag to move decorations

Hold down the left mouse button and drag items wherever you want. Moving decorations has never been easier.

---

## 4. Swap items in place

Replace a placed object with a different one and its position in space stays exactly the same.

---

## 5. Free rotation

Rotate items any way you like: horizontally, vertically, and diagonally. Build at any angle.

---

## 6. Resize items and structures

Increase or decrease the size of any item or entire structures in all building modes: guild, solo world, and the home business system.

---

## 7. Group and move objects

Group objects together and move them as a single unit. On Global, this is currently only available in the home business system.

---

## 8. No local build limit cap

The old cap of 5000 units per 50×50 m³ is gone. Build as much as you want.

**Note**: the global cap of 5000 items in the whole base is increased by 10000 in the CN version.

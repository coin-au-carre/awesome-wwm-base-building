Your guild can have multiple builds on the leaderboard. Each build has its own post with its own score, screenshots, and page. They appear grouped under your guild name.

---

## How to add a build

Create a new post in `#guild-base-showcase` named:

```
GuildName: Build Title
```

Examples: `DrunkenFist: Winter Wonderland`, `DrunkenFist: Tropical Oasis`

You have to use the same guild name! The site picks up the grouping automatically.

The older `GuildName - Build Title` format (with a dash) also works but beware of the space before and after `-`.

---

## Optional fields in your first post

You can add any of these lines anywhere in your first post:

```
Current: yes
Hosted at: LittleFury [12345678]
```

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| `Current: yes` | Marks this as the guild's active build (green tag and dot on the site) |
| `Hosted at: GuildName [ID]` | Links to the guild currently hosting this build (ID is optional) |

---

## Voting and scores

Each build has its own independent score and leaderboard rank. The grouped entry shows the **best rank** across all your builds. Clicking it expands to show each build individually.

---

## Tips

- **Don't delete old posts.** Old builds keep their votes and their page stays up.

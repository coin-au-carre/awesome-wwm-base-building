Sometimes an item refuses to highlight when you hover over it, making it impossible to delete or recycle. There are two known causes.

## Fix 1. Switch off lite client

If you are playing on the **lite client** (the lower-graphics version available on the official NetEase site), some items cannot be selected or deleted at all. The pen/inkstone (Miscellaneous category, first row) is a confirmed example.

**Solution:** switch to the standard client. Once you are no longer on lite client, the item will highlight normally and you can delete it.

## Fix 2. Use the underground camera angle

For items that still won't highlight regardless of client, the hitbox may only be reachable from below. This works for small objects placed flush with the ground (flowers, pots, etc.).

**Steps:**

1. Enter **free camera** mode.
2. Get close to the object, lower the camera as close to the ground as possible.
3. Slowly angle the camera upward so you are looking at the item from beneath the map.
4. Move very close to the item. You should see it highlight from this angle.
5. Delete or recycle it as normal.

The trick is to get the camera just at ground level and tilt it up. You may need to inch forward until the highlight appears. Once it does, the delete button works normally.

## 🗺️ What to Visit

We recommend this route to see the best of our current rotation:  
  
**The Lobby Entrance: **Home to our custom mini cat tree, the "human cat pen," and a serene Buddha shrine.  
  
**The Party Area:** Features a fully-stocked bar and interactive couple chairs. Every seat offers a different experience—results may vary! PS. You will also find a highly engineered toilet around this area with both sit and smell functionality built in.  
  
**The Arena:** A three-story complex. Relax in the lounge, visit the bar, or head to the roof for a seat right on the edge of the platform.  
  
**The Lake: **A modern architectural build with tranquil waterfront views. Visit the central pavilion to see our builders' creativity on full display.  
  
Last but not least...  
  
**The Wall: What lies beyond the wall??? You'll have to explore to find out.**

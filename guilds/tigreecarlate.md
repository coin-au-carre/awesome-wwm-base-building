## 📜 Lore

The Scarlet Tigers were born from the alliance of three brothers-in-arms, united by friendship, glory, and blood.
Forged in battle, they swore to never yield nor abandon their own.
Their crimson banner embodies every fight, every sacrifice, and their unbreakable will.
More than a guild, they are a brotherhood built on honor and strength.
Their ambition is clear: to found a dynasty that even time itself cannot erase.

## 📜 Lore

As far as i know we were the first to use main gate 4 to build castles and the first to build under the lake and of course the first guild to build a whole ass dragon

## 🗺️ What to Visit

We have a dragon, 4 castle style builds including our spectator stand, guild sparring arena, the theater and the giant castle w/throne room in the very back and we have an underwater room under the lake accessible via the cave (you have to crouch)

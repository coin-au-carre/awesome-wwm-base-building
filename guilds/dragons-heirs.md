## 📜 Lore

Dragon City is the seat of Dragons-Heirs — a living capital shaped by the will of the Divine Dragon. Built upon layered platforms of stone and discipline, it stands as both sanctuary and stronghold. Here, warriors are forged, traditions are honored, and the path of the dragon is carried forward into every battle and every moment of peace.

## 🗺️ What to Visit

**• Dragon Palace (Throne Room)**  
 — *the central seat of power overlooking the entire city*  
**• Royal Garden**   
— *a tranquil sanctuary offering peace and balance  
**• Barracks & Training Grounds **  
— where warriors are trained and prepared for war*  
**• Dragon Shrine**   
— *a sacred place honoring the Dragon God and the legacy of all dragons, offering peace and reflection*  
**• Performance Court **  
— *a vibrant space where music, gatherings, and celebrations come alive*  
**• Inn & Wedding Area **  
— *a place for rest, bonds, and new beginnings*  
**• Bar at the Party Pool **  
— *a lively place where members gather during events to relax, drink, and celebrate*  
**• Arena Food Stalls**   
— *where warriors gather strength before battle*  
**• City Roads & Bridge Platforms**   
— *paths that connect the city, used for marches and travel*  
**• Hidden Basement**  
 — *the secret domain of our Vice Master, hidden beneath the city…*

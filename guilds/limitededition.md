## 📜 Lore

Before your eyes unfolds a quiet corner of an Eastern world, where time seems to slow down and dissolve into the light. By day, the garden is filled with gentle freshness: clear water reflects elegant pavilions with carved roofs, bamboo rustles among stones and greenery, while crimson maples add a living flame to the calm palette of nature.

As evening falls, the space transforms — golden light glides across wooden railings and water, turning everything into a warm, almost dreamlike scene. Inside the pavilions, a sense of comfort and silence lingers, as if this place were meant for unhurried conversations and thoughts that require no answers.

At night, the world sinks into a deep, velvety darkness animated by soft lantern light. Their reflections shimmer on the damp stone paths, creating a feeling of warmth and gentle mystery. The space becomes more intimate, almost personal, as if it belongs only to you and that quiet glow.

This place lives in the rhythm of light — from the clear calm of day to the warm reflection of evening and the mysterious silence of night, remaining endlessly harmonious and untouched by the noise of the outside world.

## 🗺️ What to Visit

🛍️ *Left of the entrance*  
Follow the path to the trading square, where you can buy food, fabrics, and spices. A little further ahead you’ll find a visitor bar with a great view of the PvP arena — the perfect place to watch battles and discuss victories and defeats.  
  
🍵 *Behind the entrance*  
Walking down the path will lead you to one of the coziest places on the base — a peaceful lake. Here you can relax in the tea house, catch your breath in the inner bar, or even host a banquet in our “Chinese Hogwarts”.  
  
⛩️ *Up the mountain path*  
And if your soul seeks redemption after a few questionable adventures, climb the path up the hill — at the top you’ll find a temple for prayer and quiet reflection.  
  
⚔️ *Right of the entrance*  
This road leads to an additional arena for the most resilient fighters. Across from it is the entrance to a stone garden of solitude — a calm place for peaceful walks away from the bustle.  
  
💆 *Through the stone garden*  
If you continue through the garden, you’ll reach the massage salon (massage therapist positions are currently open). The spa area also includes a swimming pool, a sauna, and diving platforms for jumping competitions.  
  
✨ Our guild base was designed so that everyone can find a place here for rest, adventure, or new experiences. Welcome — and make yourself at home.
